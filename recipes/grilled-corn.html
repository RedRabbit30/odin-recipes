Grilled Fiesta Mexican Corn

Do you like Mexican food? I mean, who doesn't right? Well, do you also like corn? Well read on cause boy do I have a treat for you!

This recipe will show your friends and family how to throw down some bow chicka wow wow mexican corn! This isn't that boring corn in the can you have had in your cabinet for 3 years already. Let's do this!

Ingredients to Obtain

  • Lime Mayo
  • 6 Ears of Corn
  • 2 Limes
  • Cilantro

Cooking Steps

  1. Grill the corn
  2. In a bowl mix chopped cilantro, lime mayo and a squeeze of lime
  3. When done grilling, coat with lime mayo mix
  4. ENJOY
