Grilled Mexican Corn

Do you like Mexican food? Well, do you like corn? Well read on!

This recipe will show your friends and family how to throw down some bow chicka wow wow corn!

Ingredients to Obtain

  • Lime Mayo
  • 6 Ears of Corn
  • 2 Limes
  • Cilantro

Cooking Steps

  1. Grill the corn
  2. In a bowl mix chopped cilantro, lime mayo and a squeeze of lime
  3. When done grilling, coat with lime mayo mix
  4. ENJOY
