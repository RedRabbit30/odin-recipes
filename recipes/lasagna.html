Lasagna

How to craft the ultimate lasagna

This recipe will teach you how to craft the best damn lasagna you have ever had in your life. It will be so good your own mama will slap you into next week!

Ingredients to Obtain

  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Fresh White Whole Mushrooms
  • Prego Chunky Garden Pasta Sauce
  • Oven Ready Lasagna Noodles
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • A casserole dish

Cooking Steps

  1. In a bowl mix ricotta and mozzarella
  2. Chop mushrooms and mix into ricotta mix
  3. lay a layer of oven ready noodles
  4. Spread ricotta mix and lay some sauce
  5. Continue laying more sauce/ricotta mix
  6. Top with mozzarella cheese
  7. Bake as long as oven ready noodles say to
  8. When done let cool and gobble it up
